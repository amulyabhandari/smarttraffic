Project Description

SmartCard for Driving Licence which was recently issued in Nepal.
As we are aware about the chaotic method of the issue of chit, we have agreed in making a small prototype in which a database is created which saves the information of the licence holder and shows their history of pending chit, along with the number of traffic rule violations. Also, the information of the traffic class for the particular driver's notification is sent to their respective notification centre.
